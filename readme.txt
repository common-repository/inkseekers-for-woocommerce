Grow your store with the inkseekers apps connecting to woocommerce plugin

== Description ==

Use Inkseekers app to create and launch t-shirts & drinkware on your WooCommerce store..

Upload your designs to create t-shirt & tumbler mockups, then publish it on WooCommerce store using this app.

Our product line is more focused. Inkseeker's specialty is apparel and drinkware. Our printed t-shirts hold good in washing, are soft to touch, and have long lasting prints. 

Since our product line is more focused, by utilizing lean production processes, and optimized operation, items are embellished, quality controlled, and shipped in 2-3 business days. 

Learn more about the WooCommerce + Inkseekers integration [here](https://app.inkseekers.com).


== Installation ==
1. Upload 'inkseekers-for-woocommerce' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Click the "Connect" button after that coneecting to Inkseekers apps.
